Show how a standard LDM fails or OOMs on 6GB while yours succeeds, and how many seconds it takes to upscale a 512x512 image natively compared to Real-ESRGAN or standard Stable Diffusion upscalers.
Qualitative Metrics: Make heavy use of your integrated LPIPS and Sobel-edge loss outputs. Show zoom-in crops in your paper highlighting how the Swin layers recover textures (like eyelashes or hair, given the CelebA-HQ dataset) while the Mamba block prevents global morphing artifacts.
Final Verdict: You have an outstanding foundation. The code heavily validates the claims in your README. If you pair this with rigorous empirical testing (PSNR/SSIM/LPIPS tables) and ablations, this is a very strong candidate for a top-tier vision venue.
